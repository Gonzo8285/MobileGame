_Drafted 2026-04-30 by heartbeat. v0.1 update 2026-04-30 with Paul's three resolutions (rarity bump, Bog-Spawn draftable, Smoke + Slow promoted to formal keywords). v1.0 update 2026-04-30 — synced to Gallowfell faction names (`faction_bible.md` v1, `lore_gallowfell.md`). 3 starter factions for MVP: Iron Penitents, Ash-Mourners, Coven of the Black Mire. 18 units / 8 spells / 4 traps. Skinward Pact + The Last Legion held back as post-MVP unlocks._

## Format
- **Cost** = mana to play.
- **Units:** HP / ATK / Range / CD (cooldown in turns; 1 = attacks every turn).
- **Range:** M = melee (1 tile), S = short (2 tiles), L = long (3 tiles).
- **Rarity:** C = Common, U = Uncommon, R = Rare. (No Epic in starter pool.)
- **Keywords (formal, evergreen):** Cleave, Pierce, Bleed, Poison, Root, Fear, Shield, Resurrect, Summon, Sacrifice, Penance, Dread, **Smoke**, **Slow**.
  - **Smoke** _(zone status):_ a tile or area filled with smoke for N turns. Enemies entering or standing in Smoke suffer Fear-1 and -1 ATK while in the zone. Stacks with Dread.
  - **Slow-X** _(unit status):_ target's movement is reduced by X tiles per turn for the listed duration. Stacks with itself up to Slow-3 (rooted-equivalent). Stacks cleanly with Poison and Bleed.

## Pool totals (sanity check)
- **18 units** (6/6/6) ✓
- **8 spells** (3/2/3) ✓
- **4 traps** (0/3/1) ✓ — Ash-Mourners weighted heaviest because Control wants the trap toolkit.
- Rarity mix (v0.1): **15 C / 10 U / 5 R / 0 E** = 50% / 33% / 17%.
  - _Honest correction:_ original v0 claim of "16C/11U/3R" was a miscount — actual v0 was 15C/11U/4R. Paul asked to bump R count up; promoted M8 Cinder Tide U→R, landing at 15C/10U/5R. Five Rares = one highlight pull per faction (Iron Penitents: P6 Crucified Saint; Ash-Mourners: M6 Pyre-Priest, M8 Cinder Tide, M11 Funeral Bell; Coven of the Black Mire: C6 Mother Quag). Slightly Court-heavy on Rares — flagged for v0.2 to consider promoting C5 Briar-Hag U→R for symmetry.

## Cost curve (combined)
- 0-cost: 1 (P7)
- 1-cost: 7 (P1, P2, M1, M7, M9, C2, C7)
- 2-cost: 8 (P3, P8, M2, M3, M10, C3, C4, C8, C10) — _correction: 9; one will move to 3-cost in v0.1 if curve is too dense_
- 3-cost: 6 (P4, P9, M4, M5, M11, C9)
- 4-cost: 3 (P5, M8, C5)
- 5-cost: 2 (P6, C6)

Curve is bottom-heavy on purpose — TD starter decks need cheap plays in the first 2 waves. v0.1 tuning pass will smooth 2/3 cost density.

## Cross-faction archetype check
- **Aggro** anchor: Penitents (P4 Hammer-Confessor, P9 Procession).
- **Control** anchor: Ash-Mourners (M5 Last Censer-Bearer, M8 Cinder Tide, traps).
- **Swarm** anchor: Coven of the Black Mire (C4 Toad-Caller, C6 Mother Quag).
- Each faction has at least one card that splashes into another archetype (P5 buffs swarms of cheap Penitents → bridges to swarm; M4 Funeral Drummer Shield aura splashes into aggro; C8 Antler Crown is a sacrifice tool that talks to Penitent decks).
