# G9 — Warlord roster v1 (Gallowfell-aligned)

_Respun 2026-04-30 against `lore_gallowfell.md`. v0 used the pre-Gallowfell faction list (Ash-Mourners, Coven, Last Legion, Skinward Pact); this version uses the canonical five (Iron Penitents, The Last Legion, Ash-Mourners, Coven of the Black Mire, Skinward Pact) and ties Warlord 11 (the Innocent Saint) to the curse mechanics. Diff vs v0 at the bottom._

> **Roster shape:** 5 free + 5 paid + 1 lore-locked secret = **11 Warlords**.
> Free 5 each anchor one playstyle and one faction. Paid 5 are cross-faction or boss-specialist archetypes — flashier/synergistic, never strictly stronger. Warlord 11 is unlocked by completing a campaign with all 10 others; no IAP path. Anti-pay-to-win bound by G8.

## Free roster

### 1. Penance-Captain Vyrrun — Iron Penitents (Cathedral Ruins) — **Aggro**
- **Backstory:** Led the flagellant column from the Cathedral Ruins into Gallowfell. Believes if he bleeds enough, the gallows-tree will weep him an answer.
- **Passive — _Mortify_:** Start of each wave, lose 2 HP; your units gain +1 ATK that wave.
- **Signature unit — _Cathedral Brother_:** 1-cost, fast, low HP, **Cleave**.
- **Signature spell — _Self-Scourge_:** Deal 3 damage to your strongest unit. All your units gain +2 ATK this wave. **Per-combat cap: +6 ATK total from Self-Scourge stacking** (added 2026-05-22, CANON_PATCHES_APPLIED — round-3 wobble fix: caps Hanging Hour doubled-buff interaction at 3 meaningful casts per combat. Subsequent casts still resolve the self-damage but no further ATK buff applies. Card balance audit §19 surfaced this risk.)
- **Visual cue:** Monarchy-era execution-mask in tarnished brass, hooded, flayed back, hammer-and-rope motif on the chest plate.
- **Hanging Hour synergy:** at midnight his self-damage is doubled — and so is the buff. Risk goes vertical.

### 2. Court-Necromant Sieren — Ash-Mourners (Catacombs) — **Control**
- **Backstory:** Last court mage of the dying Monarchy. Has been signing death-warrants in her own blood for forty years; now the warrants sign back.
- **Passive — _Hanged Memory_:** The first time per battle one of your units dies, raise it on the same tile as a 1/1 Withered Servant.
- **Signature unit — _Pall-Bearer_:** mid-cost, slow, high HP, leaves Smoke on death.
- **Signature spell — _Funeral Writ_:** Root all enemies in target lane for 2 turns; deal 1 damage per turn rooted.
- **Visual cue:** court-robes gone to grave-shrouds, ink-stained fingers, raven-feather quill, no shadow.
- **Reanimation synergy:** Sieren amplifies the in-fiction Reanimation curse — _her_ rise-once is at full stats, not 1 HP.

### 3. Marsh-Mother Eddra — Coven of the Black Mire (Bog of Bargains) — **Swarm**
- **Backstory:** Bartered her firstborn for the swamp's name. Bartered the swamp's name for her seventh life. Her thirteenth body wears all the others.
- **Passive — _Brood_:** At the start of each wave, summon a free 1/1 **Familiar** (Poison-1) on your back tile.
- **Signature unit — _Bog-Born_:** 1-cost token, dies in one hit, leaves a **Poison** puddle.
- **Signature spell — _Hex of Many_:** Replace one enemy with three weak Bog-Born tokens.
- **Visual cue:** hunched, wreath of demon-coins, green pyre eyes, briar-cloak, three teeth, three shadows.

### 4. Forge-Marshal Veska — The Last Legion (The Foundry) — **Tempo**
- **Backstory:** Marshal of the Last Legion, sent to "pacify" Gallowfell six years ago. The smoke from the forges has the same iron taste as her blood now.
- **Passive — _Forge-Heat_:** When you play a Host unit, draw a card if you control 3+ Host units.
- **Signature unit — _Iron-Officer_:** mid-cost, buffs adjacent allies +1/+1, has **Echo**.
- **Signature spell — _Iron Cohort_:** All your Host units shift one tile forward, gain +1 ATK this wave.
- **Visual cue:** soot-blackened cuirass, brass officer's gorget, ironwood baton, hair bound with chain.

### 5. Tree-Walker Mhar — Skinward Pact (Cinderwood) — **Summoner**
- **Backstory:** Born at the foot of the felled god-tree. Carries its last seed in his ribcage, where his second heart used to be.
- **Passive — _Wear the Dead_:** When one of your summons dies, your next summoned unit costs 1 less.
- **Signature unit — _Cinder-Wolf_:** mid-cost, big body, **Resurrect** once.
- **Signature spell — _Don the Pelt_:** Sacrifice a unit; summon a stronger version of it on the same tile (same name, +50% stats).
- **Visual cue:** layered hide-cloak, antler-crown sprouting fresh leaves through bone, mismatched eyes, smoke trailing from his fingers.

## Paid roster

> **Unlock economy:** every paid Warlord is reachable via _Marrow Shards_ (run-end currency) **or** gems. ~12k Shards = ~12–15 hrs play. Gem prices map to the G8 IAP ladder.

### 6. The Vow-Broken Magus — _Iron Penitents × Ash-Mourners_ — **Hybrid Aggro/Control**
- **Backstory:** A Penitent who took the Court's vow of necromancy to atone faster. Both orders excommunicated him. He took it as praise.
- **Passive — _Twinned Vow_:** Every 3rd card you play, add a free random Penitent **or** Court spell to hand.
- **Signature unit — _Vow-Keeper_:** mid-cost, +1 ATK while adjacent to Smoke, deals 1 self-damage on attack.
- **Signature spell — _Brand of Penance_:** Mark an enemy. Every time the marked enemy takes damage, the next attacker (yours) gains +2 ATK.
- **Visual cue:** brass execution-mask split open down the middle to reveal a court-shroud underneath; one gloved hand, one skeletal.
- **Unlock:** 12,000 Marrow Shards **or** 1,200 gems.

### 7. Warden Caspar Voll, the Empty Throne — The Last Legion — **Tempo (boss-control)**
- **Backstory:** The Last Legion warden who jailed his own emperor for cowardice during the march on Gallowfell. The throne's been empty since. So has his face.
- **Passive — _Gaoler's Word_:** Once per battle, freeze (Slow-3) an enemy boss for 2 turns.
- **Signature unit — _Iron Shackler_:** mid-cost, applies Slow-2 on hit, **Pierce**.
- **Signature spell — _Throne Gone Cold_:** Banish a non-boss enemy to the back of the wave queue (returns later, weakened).
- **Visual cue:** keyring of human teeth, faded Ferrum sash, broken sceptre as cudgel, missing one ear.
- **Unlock:** 12,000 Marrow Shards **or** 1,200 gems.

### 8. The Saint of Gallowsmoke — _Coven of the Black Mire × Ash-Mourners_ — **Smoke synergy / swarm-control**
- **Backstory:** A Bog child accidentally canonised by the Court when they mistook her smoke for a sign. The smoke listens to her now. The Court does not know whether to bow or burn her.
- **Passive — _Drift_:** Your Smoke tiles persist 1 extra turn and deal 1 damage/turn to enemies inside.
- **Signature unit — _Censer-Wraith_:** mid-cost, **Summon:** place Smoke; **on death:** place 2 more.
- **Signature spell — _Veil of Gallowsmoke_:** Fill the entire lane with Smoke for 1 turn; all enemies inside gain **Fear-1**.
- **Visual cue:** tall thin saint-figure, halo of greenish smoke, trailing censer chains, no visible feet.
- **Unlock:** 15,000 Marrow Shards **or** 1,500 gems.

### 9. The Brass-Crowned Whelp — _Skinward Pact × Iron Penitents_ — **Summoner/Aggro**
- **Backstory:** A Cinderwood child who melted a cathedral bell and crowned himself with it. The beasts came when he wept. The Penitents came when they heard the bell.
- **Passive — _Crowned in Want_:** The first summon each wave costs 1 less and enters with **Bleed**.
- **Signature unit — _Brass Hound_:** low-cost summon, **Bleed** on hit, dies easy.
- **Signature spell — _Wail_:** Summon 3 Brass Hounds in a single tile; they rush forward, ignoring blockers for 1 turn.
- **Visual cue:** small figure in oversized brass crown, leashed pack of stitched hounds, tear-streaked, barefoot.
- **Unlock:** 18,000 Marrow Shards **or** 1,800 gems.

### 10. The Last Confessor — _Neutral / faction-flex_ — **Wildcard**
- **Backstory:** She hears every Warlord's last words. She remembers each one. She is the one nobody summons by name. She has heard yours already.
- **Passive — _Hearsay_:** Your starting deck includes 2 cards from a random rival faction each run.
- **Signature unit — _Confessor-Familiar_:** small, neutral, gains the keywords of any unit you played last turn.
- **Signature spell — _Last Words_:** Copy and re-cast the last spell used by any Warlord this run — including enemy bosses.
- **Visual cue:** blindfold, threadbare cassock, ear trumpet, walking-stick of bound prayer scrolls, mouth slightly open.
- **Unlock:** 25,000 Marrow Shards **or** 2,500 gems (~$19.99 effective).

## Lore-locked secret

### 11. The Saint That Should Not Hang — _The Curse Itself_
The wrongful execution that founded Gallowfell. She is the curse.

- **Faction:** none / all. Cannot draft cards from a single faction; her starting deck is a curated 12 from the entire pool.
- **Backstory:** Hanged in error at the founding of the gallows-town. Her death is the curse. Speaking her true name makes the gallows-tree shudder. Painters of Gallowfell paint her with eyes painted shut so her image cannot judge them.
- **Passive — _The First Wrongful Death_:** Every time the **Reanimation** curse mechanic raises an enemy, they rise fighting **for you** instead.
- **Signature unit — _Hempen Witness_:** mid-cost, immune to Reanimation, deals **double damage at the Hanging Hour**.
- **Signature spell — _Speak the Name_:** At the Hanging Hour, all units on the field stop for one turn. You play a free card.
- **Visual cue:** young woman in a noose-frayed white shift, eyes painted shut, bare feet that leave no print, faint rope-mark always visible at the throat.
- **Unlock:** complete a campaign with all 10 other Warlords. **No IAP path.** Lore-locked.
- **Why she's the secret:** she's the only Warlord who is mechanically tied to BOTH curse mechanics (Reanimation, Hanging Hour). She makes the curse work *for* the player. End-of-arc reveal.

## MVP slice (first internal build)
Ship 3 free Warlords: **Vyrrun (Aggro)**, **Sieren (Control)**, **Mhar (Summoner)**. Most distinct fantasies, validates loop variety. Eddra and Veska arrive in build 2.

## Anti-pay-to-win audit (paid roster vs G8 guardrails)
- ✅ Every paid Warlord reachable via Marrow Shards. Gems = speed only.
- ✅ No paid passive is strictly better than a free passive — different fantasies, not better numbers.
- ✅ Cross-faction Warlords give *flexibility*, not *raw power*.
- ✅ The Last Confessor priced highest because she's the most _interesting_, not strongest.
- ✅ Warlord 11 (Innocent Saint) is **explicitly no-IAP**. The end-of-arc payoff stays earned.

## Hanging Hour & Reanimation hooks (per `lore_gallowfell.md`)
Lore-mechanics surface in three Warlords by design:
- **Vyrrun (free):** Hanging Hour doubles his self-damage AND his buff — vertical risk.
- **Sieren (free):** her Hanged Memory raises units at full stats, amplifying the curse's Reanimation.
- **The Saint That Should Not Hang (Warlord 11):** Reanimated enemies turn into your units, AND her Speak the Name spell triggers at the Hanging Hour. She *is* the curse, weaponised.

The other 8 Warlords interact with Hanging Hour and Reanimation through generic +ATK / on-death effects, but only these three have *signature* synergies. Keeps the lore mechanics meaningful but not noisy.
