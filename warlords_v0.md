# G9 — Warlord roster v0 (10 characters)

_Drafted 2026-04-30 by heartbeat. 5 free at launch span all five playstyles so any new player finds their fit. 5 paid Warlords are flashier/synergistic but never strictly stronger — every paid Warlord is also reachable via grind (Marrow Shards). Anti-pay-to-win bound by G8._

## Free roster (5 — one per playstyle, one per faction)

### 1. Brother Ardent — Iron Penitents — **Aggro**
- **Backstory:** A flagellant captain who kept marching after his faith broke. Pain is the only thing he still trusts.
- **Passive — _Mortify_:** At the start of each wave, lose 2 HP; your units gain +1 ATK that wave.
- **Signature unit — _Penitent Brother_:** 1-cost, low HP, fast, **Cleave**.
- **Signature spell — _Self-Scourge_:** Deal 3 damage to your strongest unit. All your units gain +2 ATK this wave.
- **Visual cue:** Hooded, brass mask, hammer-and-chain, flayed back, ash on the boots.
- **Why he's the new-player aggro pick:** the loop teaches itself — hurt yourself, hit harder, win or die fast. Easy to grasp the snowball.

### 2. Mater Cinderis — Ash-Mourners — **Control**
- **Backstory:** An undertaker who sang the wrong dirge. Now the burned dead won't stay buried.
- **Passive — _Long Vigil_:** The first time per battle one of your units dies, place a Smoke tile in its lane.
- **Signature unit — _Pall-Bearer_:** mid-cost, slow, high HP, leaves Smoke on death.
- **Signature spell — _Funeral Rites_:** Root all enemies in target lane for 2 turns; deal 1 damage per turn rooted.
- **Visual cue:** ash-grey robes, censer trailing smoke, twin black veil over the eyes, no shadow.
- **Why she's the control pick:** rewards thinking 2 waves ahead — Smoke + Root + Burn synergies you can read on the board.

### 3. Hag Murielle — Coven of the Black Mire — **Swarm**
- **Backstory:** The youngest of the Coven, thirteen lifetimes ago. Hasn't slept since the third.
- **Passive — _Brood_:** At the start of each wave, summon a free 1/1 **Familiar** (Poison-1) on your back tile.
- **Signature unit — _Mire-Born_:** 1-cost token, dies in one hit, leaves a **Poison** puddle (1 dmg/turn for 3 turns to anything that walks it).
- **Signature spell — _Hex of Many_:** Replace one enemy unit with three weak Mire-Born tokens (yes, really).
- **Visual cue:** hunched, wreath of toad-skulls, green pyre eyes, briar-cloak, three teeth.
- **Why she's the swarm pick:** trash-board-trash-board, lots of clicks, lots of poison ticking — feels great if "more bodies" is your love language.

### 4. Magister Vellenne — The Last Legion — **Tempo**
- **Backstory:** Imperial battle-mage. The empire fell on her watch. She intends to outlive the verdict.
- **Passive — _Standard-Bearer_:** When you play a Legion unit, draw a card if you control 3+ Legion units.
- **Signature unit — _Banner-Lieutenant_:** mid-cost, buffs adjacent allies +1/+1, has **Echo** (replays once on resolve).
- **Signature spell — _Cohort Step_:** All your Legion units shift one tile forward and gain +1 ATK this wave.
- **Visual cue:** tattered crimson cloak, broken signum staff, half-burnt sigil-tattoo down one cheek.
- **Why she's the tempo pick:** drawing cards while playing them feels good. Teaches the engine concept (build a deck that builds itself) without a full deckbuilder lecture.

### 5. Vren the Stitched — Skinward Pact — **Summoner**
- **Backstory:** Wears the pelts of every Pact-elder he's ever buried. Most of them volunteered. Most.
- **Passive — _Wear the Dead_:** When one of your summons dies, your next summoned unit costs 1 less.
- **Signature unit — _Marrow-Wolf_:** mid-cost, big body, **Resurrect** once.
- **Signature spell — _Don the Pelt_:** Sacrifice one of your units; summon a stronger version of that unit on the same tile (same name, +50% stats).
- **Visual cue:** layered hide-cloak, wolfskin mantle, antler-crown, bone necklace clattering, mismatched eyes.
- **Why he's the summoner pick:** the death-into-summon loop is the entire fantasy in one passive. Big bodies, big losses, bigger comebacks.

## Paid roster (5 — flashy, synergy-driven, never strictly stronger)

> **Unlock economy:** every paid Warlord is reachable via _Marrow Shards_ (run-end currency) **or** gems. Shard rate ≈ 1,000/run-win at Asc-1 → 12k Shards = ~12–15 hrs play. Gem rate maps to the G8 IAP ladder (1,200 gems ≈ $9.99 SKU).

### 6. The Marrow-Sage — _cross-faction: Iron Penitents × Ash-Mourners_ — **Hybrid Aggro/Control**
- **Backstory:** A Penitent who took the Mourner's vow. Both orders excommunicated him. He took it as praise.
- **Passive — _Twinned Vow_:** Every 3rd card you play, add a free random Penitent **or** Mourner spell to your hand.
- **Signature unit — _Vow-Keeper_:** mid-cost, +1 ATK while adjacent to Smoke, deals 1 self-damage per attack.
- **Signature spell — _Brand of Repentance_:** Mark an enemy. Every time the marked enemy takes damage, the next attacker (yours) gains +2 ATK.
- **Visual cue:** Penitent mask split down the middle to reveal an Ash-Mourner veil underneath; one bare arm flayed, one swathed in ash-cloth.
- **Synergy hook:** lets you splash both factions in one deck — premium *flexibility*, not premium *power*.
- **Unlock:** 12,000 Marrow Shards **or** 1,200 gems (~$9.99 effective).

### 7. Old Gaol — The Last Legion — **Tempo with control twist**
- **Backstory:** A Legion warden who jailed his own emperor for cowardice. The throne's been empty since.
- **Passive — _Gaoler's Word_:** Once per battle, freeze (Slow-3) an enemy boss for 2 turns.
- **Signature unit — _Iron Shackler_:** mid-cost, applies Slow-2 on hit, **Pierce**.
- **Signature spell — _Empty Throne_:** Banish a non-boss enemy to the back of the wave queue (it returns later, weakened).
- **Visual cue:** keyring of human teeth, faded imperial sash, broken sceptre carried as a cudgel, missing one ear.
- **Synergy hook:** dictates pace against bosses. Skill ceiling on _when_ to spend the freeze; skill floor not punishingly high.
- **Unlock:** 12,000 Marrow Shards **or** 1,200 gems.

### 8. Saint of Smoke — _cross-faction: Coven × Ash-Mourners_ — **Smoke synergy / swarm-control**
- **Backstory:** A child of the Mire who was canonised by accident. The Smoke listens to her.
- **Passive — _Drift_:** Your Smoke tiles persist 1 extra turn and deal 1 damage/turn to enemies inside.
- **Signature unit — _Censer-Wraith_:** mid-cost, **Summon:** place Smoke; **on death:** place 2 more.
- **Signature spell — _Veil of Smoke_:** Fill the entire lane with Smoke for 1 turn; all enemies inside gain **Fear-1**.
- **Visual cue:** tall, thin saint-figure, halo of greenish smoke, trailing censer chains, no visible feet.
- **Synergy hook:** mainline the Smoke keyword into a real archetype. Makes the keyword *worth* having across two factions.
- **Unlock:** 15,000 Marrow Shards **or** 1,500 gems (~$14.99 effective).

### 9. The Boy-King in Brass — _cross-faction: Skinward Pact × Iron Penitents_ — **Summoner/Aggro hybrid**
- **Backstory:** A child who crowned himself with a melted bell. The beasts came when he wept. They never left.
- **Passive — _Crowned in Want_:** The first summon each wave costs 1 less and enters with **Bleed**.
- **Signature unit — _Brass Hound_:** low-cost summon, **Bleed** on hit, dies to a stiff breeze.
- **Signature spell — _Wail_:** Summon 3 Brass Hounds in a single tile; they immediately rush forward, ignoring blockers for 1 turn.
- **Visual cue:** small figure in an over-large brass crown, leashed pack of stitched hounds, tear-streaked, barefoot.
- **Synergy hook:** glass-cannon swarm — high damage, high churn, demands skilled placement to survive.
- **Unlock:** 18,000 Marrow Shards **or** 1,800 gems.

### 10. The Last Confessor — _neutral / faction-flex_ — **Utility / wildcard**
- **Backstory:** She hears every Warlord's last words. She remembers each one. She is the one nobody summons by name.
- **Passive — _Hearsay_:** Your starting deck includes 2 cards drawn from a random rival faction each run.
- **Signature unit — _Confessor-Familiar_:** small, neutral, gains the keywords of any unit you played last turn.
- **Signature spell — _Last Words_:** Copy and re-cast the last spell used by any Warlord this run — including enemy bosses.
- **Visual cue:** blindfold, threadbare cassock, ear trumpet, walking-stick of bound prayer scrolls, mouth permanently slightly open.
- **Synergy hook:** the "expert" pick. More _interesting_ than strictly stronger — variance is the fantasy. Steep learning curve, infinite replay variance.
- **Unlock:** 25,000 Marrow Shards **or** 2,500 gems (~$19.99 effective). Premium price reflects identity, not power.

## MVP slice for first internal build (per GDD)
First playable build ships **3 of the free 5**: Brother Ardent (aggro), Mater Cinderis (control), Vren the Stitched (summoner). That covers the three most distinct fantasies and validates the variety of the loop. Hag Murielle and Magister Vellenne arrive in build 2 once swarm AI and draw-engine UI are stable.

## Anti-pay-to-win audit (paid roster vs G8 guardrails)
- ✅ Every paid Warlord is reachable via Marrow Shards. Gems = speed only.
- ✅ No paid passive is strictly better than a free passive — they're _different fantasies_, not _better numbers_.
- ✅ Cross-faction Warlords give *flexibility*, not *raw power* (e.g. Marrow-Sage gives 1 free spell every 3 cards — *useful*, not *broken*).
- ✅ The Last Confessor is the priciest because she's the most _interesting_, not the strongest. RNG-flavoured. Skilled players love her; new players bounce.
- ✅ No paid Warlord locks a card or relic out of the free pool. Their signature units/spells are visual identity, not power-walled mechanics.
