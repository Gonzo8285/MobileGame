# G5 — Working title candidates

## The 5 candidates

### 1. Gallowmarch ⭐ (heartbeat top pick)
- **Vibe:** A doomed army marches under the gallows. Grimdark, percussive, original.
- **App-store collision risk:** None found in iOS/Play search. No mobile game with this name surfaced.
- **Domain:** `gallowmarch.com` likely available (no game/brand currently using it as of last public WHOIS-ish hits — needs registrar check). `.io`, `.gg`, `.app` all probably free.
- **Trademark risk:** No matches in casual search. Compound of two common English words → strong distinctive mark on game-class TM.
- **Pronounceable, memorable, ASO-friendly:** 11 chars, fits Apple 30-char title easily.
- **Suggested store title:** `Gallowmarch: Roguelite TD`

### 2. Penitent Lane
- **Vibe:** Religious-flagellant pun on "tower-defence lane." Unique. Slight irony fits gallows-humour mandate.
- **Collision risk:** Clean. No hits.
- **Domain:** `penitentlane.com` likely free.
- **Trademark risk:** Low.
- **Concern:** "Lane" reads a bit web-2.0 / cosy; might undersell grimdark vibe. Also less Google-able due to common English bigram.
- **Suggested store title:** `Penitent Lane: Grimdark TD`

### 3. The Last Banner
- **Vibe:** Doomed last-stand imagery. Strong in fantasy.
- **Collision risk:** ⚠️ **Close clash:** "Tattered Banners" exists on Steam. "Last Banner" is generic enough that TM may struggle. Crusader Kings / Total War / countless fantasy uses of "banner".
- **Domain:** `lastbanner.com` almost certainly taken or expensive.
- **Recommendation:** Drop this one.

### 4. Ashen Tide
- **Vibe:** Atmospheric, painterly.
- **Collision risk:** ⚠️ **High:** "Ashen Legacy" (App Store) and "Ashen" (mobile) both exist. Same audience, near-identical hooks. Confusion likely.
- **Recommendation:** Drop this one.

### 5. Hollow Crown
- **Vibe:** Shakespearean, doomed-king imagery, fits faction "The Last Legion".
- **Collision risk:** ⚠️ **High:** "Hollow Knight" is a top-tier indie. Audience confusion on store search is near-certain. Also a BBC TV series.
- **Recommendation:** Drop this one.
