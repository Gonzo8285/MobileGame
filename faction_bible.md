# Faction Bible v1 — Gallowfell-aligned

_Rewritten 2026-04-30 to align with `lore_gallowfell.md`. v0 used pre-Gallowfell names (Ash-Mourners, Coven of the Black Mire, The Last Legion, Skinward Pact); this version uses the canonical five. Diff vs v0 at the bottom._

5 factions for **The Curse of Gallowfell**. Each faction has a different reason to march into the cursed gallows-town. No GW / WotC / Privateer Press names — IP boundary cross-checked.

Each faction = **vibe** (one line) + **visual cue** (silhouette + colour + motif) + **mechanical role** (anchors deckbuild) + **flagship unit** (signature card to make the faction recognisable on first look) + **biome** (chapter map district) + **why they march on Gallowfell**.

---

## 1. Iron Penitents — Cathedral Ruins
- **Vibe:** Self-flagellant crusaders who believe the world is being punished for sin and that suffering is the only currency the gods accept. Aggro through martyrdom — they hurt themselves to hurt the lane harder.
- **Visual cue:** Rust-red robes over scarred bare backs; faceless iron helmets shaped like nails; chains dragging from wrists; banners stitched from old prayer scrolls. Palette: oxblood, dull iron, candle-yellow.
- **Motif:** Hammer + chain. Spilled wax. Bleeding crowns.
- **Mechanical role:** **Aggro / sacrifice.** Cheap units, self-damage triggers, stacking BLEED + PENANCE keywords. High floor, no defence.
- **Flagship unit:** _Nail-Choir Flagellant_ — 1-cost, gains +1 ATK each time a friendly Penitent dies this run.
- **Biome:** Cathedral Ruins. Prayer gone silent; pews split; the bell-tower still rings on its own.
- **Why they march on Gallowfell:** Pilgrimage. The Monarchy's national gallows is the holiest site of suffering they know. They believe flagellant prayer will free the trapped souls.

## 2. Ash-Mourners — Catacombs
- **Vibe:** Necromancer-aristocrats — the Monarchy's last court, clinging to power by draining the death-essence of the gallows-town's ceaseless executions. Imperial decay, perfumed rot, polite menace. Control through warrant and grave.
- **Visual cue:** Black brocade court-wear gone half-rotten; gilt-bone masks; signet rings on skeletal fingers; ravens on every shoulder; ink-stained gloves. Palette: bruise-purple, tarnished gold, grave-grey.
- **Motif:** Crown + warrant. Black writs. Sealed bone.
- **Mechanical role:** **Control / debuff.** Slow-Root-Smoke-Fear chains; raise-from-dead synergies (hook into the lore Reanimation curse). **Primary keyword: PERSIST** (M1, 2026-05-10) — Ash-Mourner units that die return at end-of-turn at -1 ATK, once per combat. Stacks alongside existing Smoke / Resurrect. See `keywords/persist_v0.md`.
- **Flagship unit:** _Lord-Justiciar Vey, Witness in Black_ — 4-cost, when an enemy dies in your lane, summon a 1/1 Withered Servant on the same tile.
- **Biome:** Catacombs. The Monarchy's mass grave; corridors of standing dead, sealed with court signets.
- **Why they march on Gallowfell:** They *are* the Monarchy's last court. Returning to reclaim the death-essence they've drained for centuries. Without it, the court ends.

## 3. Coven of the Black Mire — Bog of Bargains
- **Vibe:** Bog-folk who bartered with the demon that walks the curse. Half-divine, half-debt-collector. Their patron leaves a hoofprint and they all step in it. Swarm through bargain.
- **Visual cue:** Bog-mud skin under tattoos; bone-white teeth; leech-belts; demon-coin pendants; third eyes painted on; familiars (toads, eels, half-rotted children) with one too many limbs. Palette: bog-green, demon-coin gold, leech-purple.
- **Motif:** Coin + briar. Cloven hoofprint in the mud. Bound dolls.
- **Mechanical role:** **Swarm / poison.** Cheap tokens (Bog-Spawn 0/1), POISON stacks, SACRIFICE → summon-bigger.
- **Flagship unit:** _Mother Quag, Thirteen-Times-Drowned_ — 5-cost, every 3rd enemy killed this turn re-enters lane as a Bog-Spawn for you.
- **Biome:** Bog of Bargains. Demon-touched marsh at the wall's edge; coins floating face-up in the mud; the water remembers names.
- **Why they march on Gallowfell:** The curse opened a thin spot in reality. Their patron demon walks Gallowfell now. They follow it like dogs.

## 4. The Last Legion — The Foundry
- **Vibe:** The dying empire's regiment, sent to "secure" Gallowfell. Industrial, soot-stained, stubborn. The forges they ran never went cold; the curse keeps them stoked. Discipline through routine, even after meaning has gone. Tempo through formation, not flash.
- **Visual cue:** Blackened steel cuirasses with brass plate-rivets; soot-painted faces; factory aprons over uniform; standards in iron, not cloth; **Crowned Anvil sigil**. No officer above captain — every general is dead. Palette: soot-black, brass, ember-orange.
- **Motif:** Anvil + bayonet. Forge-spark. Folded iron standard.
- **Mechanical role:** **Tempo / formation.** Adjacency buffs, cheap card draw on kill, RALLY + ECHO keywords (units in a line gain +1 ATK; on-resolve replays).
- **Flagship unit:** _Sergeant-Smith Vikar, the Iron Watch_ — 4-cost, all friendly Host units in the same lane row gain +1 ATK and SHIELD-1.
- **Biome:** The Foundry. Black-coal forges still burn; iron rails crisscross the streets; the smoke is the same iron taste as Host blood now.
- **Why they march on Gallowfell:** A regiment sent by the dying empire to "secure" the town. The curse is in their lungs now, and they can't leave.
